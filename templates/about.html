About Page

Imgur is a popular image-hosting site, where individuals can share images with one another without the need of creating an account. However, what if there was a similar system that allows individuals to easily share audio files? Although services like Google Drive and DropBox may allow sharing of audio files, they require users to create an account beforehand. With our service, Audur, individuals upload an audio file (.mp3, .mp4, .wav, etc.) onto the web and create a shareable link that others can access.
